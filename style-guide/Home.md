# Frontdesko Style Guide

Welcome to the official style guide for Frontdesko — a cloud-based system that simplifies hotel operations.

This style guide defines the language, tone, and terminology used across Frontdesko’s product interface, website content, support material, and all customer-facing communication. The purpose of this guide is to help everyone at Frontdesko write in a clear, consistent way. 

Whether we’re writing UI text, emails, help docs, or marketing content, this guide ensures we all sound like one brand, not many different voices. By following this guide, we make our product more usable, our brand more memorable, and our communication more efficient across teams.

## Who this guide is for

This guide is intended for anyone who creates content or communication for Frontdesko, including:

- **UX/UI writers** – writing in-product text, labels, and messages  
- **Technical writers** – creating help docs, FAQs, and knowledge base content  
- **Product designers** – writing microcopy for screens, forms, and flows  
- **Developers** – adding system messages, alerts, or API-visible text  
- **Marketing & brand teams** – creating website content, social posts, and campaigns  
- **Customer support & onboarding teams** – writing emails, chat replies, and tutorials  
- **Founders, partners, and external collaborators** – ensuring brand alignment in all communication

## What this guide includes

This style guide is divided into the following key sections:

- **Voice & Tone** – how Frontdesko speaks as a brand  
- **Writing Rules** – grammar, punctuation, spelling, and sentence style  
- **Terminology** – approved terms, phrases to avoid, and hospitality glossary  
- **UI Text** – patterns for buttons, forms, errors, alerts, and microcopy  
- **Content Types** – standards for product UI, website, help docs, emails, and marketing  
- **Visual Style** – logo usage, color palette, typography, and image guidelines  
- **Ownership** – who maintains this guide and how updates are managed
